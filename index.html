Hi! ​
I am Xiang Li (李想), a PhD student at School of Computing, National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Kenji Kawaguchi. Prior to NUS, I completed my B.E. in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) in June 2022, advised by Prof. Ruifeng Xu.
My research interests focus on trustworthy generative artificial intelligence — especially copyright violation and protection in diffusion models and large language models. I am also interested in exploring the memorization and creativity ability of generative models.
